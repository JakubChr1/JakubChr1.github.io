About Us

Graphic design has undoubtedly been an integral part of our the modern world. But what about the world after this one? This is the big question our colective tries to answer. What is the future of graphic design in a post apocalyptic future.

Our key points include:

Preserving Information and Knowledge

Fostering Human Connection

Building a Visual Identity

Sustainability and Resource Management

Inspiring Creativity and Hope
